about us

success with unmatched expertise in agricultural machinery & service

Established in 2018, Machinetech Solutions & Services (KE) Limited is a premier company dedicated to providing exceptional aftersales support services in agriculture. With machinery and plant equipment, our mission is to enhance the productivity and efficiency of our clients through high-quality machinery and comprehensive service solutions.
Machinetech Solutions & Services (KE) Limited is an appointed Nairobi Dealership for Solis Kenya.
Our people are the cornerstone of what makes the Solis and Machinetech Solutions & Services (KE) Limited partnership unique, driving a distinctive approach rooted in entrepreneurship. Together, we deliver a superior customer experience by leveraging the right digital channels to engage effectively with each customer segment. We are committed to building long-term business relationships, which is why every interaction with our customers and suppliers is thoughtfully cultivated. At the heart of this partnership is our shared belief that people are the foundation of our business.

our services

how we empower your agricultural success

tractor sales

We offer a comprehensive range of high-performance tractors tailored to meet your agricultural and industrial needs. Our expert team provides professional consultations to help you choose the right equipment, and we handle everything from seamless installations to routine maintenance and efficient repair services, ensuring your tractors operate at peak performance year-round.

generator sales

We offer a comprehensive range of high-quality generators tailored to your power needs. Our expert team handles professional installations and provides regular maintenance and efficient repair services to ensure your generators run smoothly.

tractors & combines
maintenance and repairs

Our skilled technicians specialize in maintaining and repairing tractors and combines. From routine servicing to complex repairs, we ensure your agricultural machines are in optimal condition, maximizing their performance and longevity.

part sales

Explore our wide selection of genuine parts for various agricultural and plant machinery. Sourced from reputable manufacturers, our parts guarantee quality and reliability, helping you keep your machines operating at their best.

fire engines
maintenance and repairs

We specialize in the maintenance and repair of fire engines. Our experienced technicians service and repair all types of fire pumps, ensuring reliable fire protection systems for our customers.

general plant maintainance

Our comprehensive maintenance services cover various types of plant machinery. Our technicians are equipped to handle routine maintenance, inspections, and repairs, ensuring your equipment operates efficiently and reduces downtime.

Our knowledgeable consultants provide valuable insights and guidance in the agricultural industry. Whether you need advice on farming techniques, crop selection, or equipment optimization, we support your success. Additionally, we offer a range of agricultural supplies to meet your needs.
